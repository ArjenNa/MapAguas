About

Arjen Naafs

Senior WASH Specialist · MSc Hydrogeology, Free University Amsterdam

mapAguas is the independent practice of Arjen Naafs, a Dutch hydrogeologist and WASH specialist with 25+ years of experience working across Africa, South Asia, and beyond. After building and leading a successful consultancy in Mozambique and serving as Climate Lead and Head of M&E at IRC WASH, Arjen launched mapAguas full-time in 2026.

The practice combines deep technical groundwater expertise with strategic programme management, data intelligence, and a long track record in knowledge translation — turning field evidence into tools, guidance, and actionable insight for governments, NGOs, and international partners.

He is proud to be an Associate Consultant to IRC WASH and a supporting specialist to WE Consult Mozambique
